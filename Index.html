Our Love Story

Two hearts, one destiny. Discover how our paths crossed and our love story began.

Nshuti's Perspective

From the first moment I saw Inshuti, something inside me changed. She wasn't just beautiful—she had this calm, kind energy that made everything around her feel lighter.

"She's the one," I told myself quietly.

I didn't push or chase. I respected her space, waited patiently, and kept her in my prayers. I believed if it was meant to be, the moment would come.

And it did. When I finally opened my heart to her, she looked at me with a gentle smile and said, "I feel the same."

Inshuti's Perspective

He was so... silent. I hated it at first. I was used to boys who tried to impress me. Nshuti didn't even try. He just did the work—carried water, fixed broken things, stood in the background like he didn't matter.

But somehow, I noticed him more than anyone.

I started to fall for the way he listened. The way he never tried to change me. The way he looked at me like I was enough.

"Nshuti and Inshuti—our names even sound like they were meant to belong together."
